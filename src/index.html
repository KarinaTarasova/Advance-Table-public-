This application will allow you to see the full picture of your budget and will be useful, first of all to those who have piece-work.
You can save the value of the earned and received money on a certain date.

Key features:

  1. Save salary, advances, bonuses and benefits.
  2. Create reporting periods - you will be shown a general summary for each period and you will be able to keep several periods at the same time.
  3. Cloud storage, synchronization. Easily transfer your data from one device to another and do not be afraid to lose important income salary.
  4. Use one account from multiple devices.
  5. Offline - it is not necessary to use an account, the data will be stored only on the device.
  6. Calculator is built-in, calculate the salary directly in the application!
  7. Do not forget to enter data-a flexible system of settings allows you to create a schedule of reminders.
  8. Currencies - select the currency that will be displayed in statistic.
  9. Feedback - is available directly from the application. Choose the method that is good for you and do not hesitate to write. The application is under construction and your feedback and wishes will help to make it more convenient and useful.
